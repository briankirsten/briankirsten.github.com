--- layout: post title: "Search Engines for Handwritten Documents" --- "Researchers at the University of Massachusetts have created a tool for automatically searching handwritten historical documents, such as the 140,000 pages that make up George Washington's personal papers in the Library of Congress. The most interesting part is that the papers are scanned versions of the originals and the search tool actually recognizes the handwritten text from these images." http://ciir.cs.umass.edu/research/wordspotting
