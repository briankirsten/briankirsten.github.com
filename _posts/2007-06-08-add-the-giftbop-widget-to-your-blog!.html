--- layout: post title: "Add the GiftBop widget to your blog!" --- First off I should probably explain what GiftBop is. GiftBop is a gift list website, for telling folks what you desire most in the world (that can be purchased). Head on over there and start building your list, it's FREE! For folks who have websites of their own they can add the GiftBop widget to show off what people are asking for.
