--- layout: post title: "Homing Pigeons faster than ADSL" --- A group of Israeli scientists decided to test whether or not the PEI (Pigeon Enabled Internet) is faster than ADSL. They attached 4GB of data to 3 pigeons and had them fly 100km. The result? The pigeons transfered the data at 2.27Mbs versus the average 1.5 Mbs that ADSL offers.
