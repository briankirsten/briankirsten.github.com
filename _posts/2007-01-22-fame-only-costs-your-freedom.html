--- layout: post title: "Fame only costs your freedom" --- Brain Scan Studios proudly presents One Last Song, a six issue black and white mini-series from writer CJ Hurtt and artist Shawn Richter. In 2046, American media personalities must obtain an officially sanctioned "performance card" or face internment in "terror aversion camps". Musician Amanda Casey is a rebel spreading the truth about her government's corruption via seemingly innocent love songs. What happens when she is found out? One Last Song #1 is currently available to order in the February edition of Diamond's Previews using order code FEB073275. It will be in stores April 2007, the rest of the series will be bimonthly. For more information and preview pages please visit the official One Last Song website.
