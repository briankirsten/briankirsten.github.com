--- layout: post title: "MySpace and music videos" --- One of the things that impressed me initially with MySpace is the music player "widget" they've got all over the site. The player's using Flash to do it's magic, so it works on my machine (a Mac), my wife's machine, nearly everyone's machine. So I nearly fell over in shock when I clicked to watch Gnarls Barkley's video for Crazy, their single off GB's first album St. Elsewhere and received this in the pop-up: "Sorry, we currently only support Internet Explorer browsers with Windows Media Player 9+" Ummm....MySpace just a little tip....Flash+Video=Works really well (See YouTube).
