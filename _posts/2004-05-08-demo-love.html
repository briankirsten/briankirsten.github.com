--- layout: post title: "Demo love" --- A bunch of folks in the comics blogosphere has been writing about this, so I figured I should too in order to spread the word. Sponsors too numerous to mention are holding a Demo Contest for Brian Wood and Becky Cloonan's Demo, a wonderful little series about having "super" powers but not becoming a "super" hero in the "conventional" sense. It's like the anti-X-men. Good stuff, should be read by all. Enter the contest today and if you haven't read it, bother your local retailer to carry it.
